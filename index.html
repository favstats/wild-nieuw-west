How to Play

Overview

Wild Nieuw-West is a murder mystery party game. Players take on secret roles and move through the house completing tasks. Murderers try to "kill" guests in the dark. Detectives try to catch them. Innocents try to survive.

Roles

  • Murderer Secretly eliminate guests during lights-out rounds. You need 20 kills to win.
  • Detective Investigate and find the murderers. Watch, listen, and deduce.
  • Innocent Survive and help identify the murderers through voting.

Tasks & Rooms

Every 20 minutes, you'll receive a new task sending you to a specific room. Go there and complete the social task. This keeps everyone moving and creates opportunities for murder.

Lights Out

Periodically, the host will call "Lights Out!" Everyone closes their eyes. Murderers silently tap a victim on the shoulder. When lights return, murdered players press "I Have Been Murdered!" on their phone.

Discussion & Voting

After a murder is discovered, players discuss suspicions and may vote to "arrest" a suspect. If the group correctly arrests a murderer, that murderer is eliminated.

Victory

  • Murderers win if they reach 20 kills.
  • Innocents & Detectives win if all murderers are arrested before reaching 20 kills.
